Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Yelena Inventory is an inventory management application developed by Lior Shmila. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information when you use it.

Yelena Inventory is currently designed to work without user registration, login, advertising, analytics, or tracking services.

2. Information We Collect

Yelena Inventory does not collect personal information from users.

The application does not require an account, does not ask users to register or log in, and does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, or other personal identifiers for external processing.

The application is used to record inventory-related information, such as branches, employees, barcode records, quantities, audit entries, and optional product images. This information is currently stored locally on the user's device.

3. Camera Permission

Yelena Inventory requests access to the device camera only for barcode scanning.

The camera is used to detect and read barcodes during inventory operations. Camera access is not used for advertising, analytics, tracking, or any unrelated purpose. Images from the camera are not uploaded to a server by the current version of the application.

If you choose not to grant camera permission, you may still enter barcodes manually or use an external barcode scanner that works as keyboard input.

4. Local Data Storage

Inventory data is currently stored locally on the device. This may include inventory records, branch and employee records, audit logs, barcode identifiers, quantities, and optional product images associated with barcodes.

Product images, when added by the user, are stored in the application's private storage on the device. They are optional and are used only to help visually identify items during inventory work.

Uninstalling the application or clearing its app data may remove locally stored information.

5. Future Cloud Synchronization

Future versions of Yelena Inventory may add synchronization with a central server for inventory or product information.

Cloud synchronization is not part of the current application behavior. If server synchronization or remote data processing is introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what information is synchronized, how it is used, and what choices users have.

6. Third-Party Services

Yelena Inventory does not use advertising services.

Yelena Inventory does not use analytics or tracking services, including Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or similar tracking SDKs.

The application may use standard platform and open-source software components to provide app functionality, such as barcode scanning, local storage, and image selection. These components are used only to support the application's features and are not used by Yelena Inventory to collect personal information for advertising or analytics.

7. Data Security

Because inventory data is currently stored locally on the device, protecting access to the device also helps protect the information stored by the application.

Yelena Inventory is designed to keep its local application data within the app's storage area. However, no method of digital storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Yelena Inventory is intended for business inventory management and is not directed to children.

The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the application, legal requirements, or future functionality.

If cloud synchronization, user accounts, analytics, or other data-related features are introduced in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
