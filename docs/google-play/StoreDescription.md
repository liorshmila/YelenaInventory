## Full Description

Yelena Inventory is a professional inventory management app for accurate barcode-based stock counting on Android. Built for real business operations, it helps teams record inventory activity with a clear, reliable workflow from branch selection to audit history.

Designed for retail stores, warehouses, distribution centers, and small to medium businesses, Yelena Inventory gives inventory teams a structured way to select a branch, choose an employee, scan or enter a barcode, record quantities, and review completed activity.

The app is built for focused inventory work, with barcode scanning, manual barcode entry, product image support, and a clean Material Design interface that keeps the counting process simple and organized.

### Key Benefits

Yelena Inventory helps businesses complete inventory checks with less friction. The workflow is direct and repeatable: select the branch, select the employee, scan the barcode, enter the quantity, and continue to the next item.

The application works offline using a local database, making it suitable for stores, warehouses, and stock areas where internet access may be limited or unreliable.

### Main Features

- Barcode scanning for inventory counting
- Manual barcode entry support

- Offline operation with local device storage

### Why Choose Yelena Inventory

Yelena Inventory focuses on the daily needs of inventory employees and stock controllers. It avoids unnecessary setup, accounts, subscriptions, advertisements, and tracking. The workflow stays close to the way inventory is handled in the field: choose the right branch, identify the employee, scan the item, record the quantity, and keep moving.

Product images help employees quickly confirm they are counting the correct item, reducing mistakes during inventory work. They are optional and never interrupt the counting process.

### Current Availability

Yelena Inventory currently stores inventory information locally on the device and does not require cloud synchronization, user accounts, analytics, advertisements, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.

Reliable, fast, and organized, Yelena Inventory gives businesses a practical Android app for everyday inventory management and barcode-based stock operations.
